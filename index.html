Kun-Ying Lee

Undergraduate Student at Tsinghua University.

I am a first-year undergraduate student in the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Tsinghua University.

Currently, I am a research intern at the MARS Lab at the Institute for Interdisciplinary Information Sciences (IIIS), advised by Prof. Hang Zhao. My research interests lie broadly in Robotics and Embodied AI.

Additionally, I am a member of the TH-MOS (Tsinghua Master of Soccer) robot soccer team, working on Gait Control algorithms for competitive robots.

Outside of research, I am a passionate pianist and a member of the Tsinghua University Clavier Team. I also enjoy playing frisbee with the CS department team.
